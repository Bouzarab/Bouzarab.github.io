FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free trial?

Yes! OpenSEM Pro comes with a 3-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required.

Can I use it on both Mac and Windows?

Yes. Your license works on one machine at a time, and we provide native installers for both macOS and Windows.

How does it compare to SmartPLS?

OpenSEM Pro includes all the PLS-SEM and CB-SEM features you'd expect (bootstrap, IPMA, MGA, FIMIX, NCA, HTMT, and more), plus comprehensive statistical analysis tools (like SPSS), and qualitative textual analysis (like NVivo) — all in one tool at a fraction of the price.

Do I need an internet connection?

An internet connection is required for license activation and periodic verification. Once activated, the software works offline for your day-to-day analysis.

Do you offer academic or institutional pricing?

Yes! We offer volume licensing for universities and institutions. Contact us at contact@opensempro.com for a custom quote.
